Guided warm-up

Every step timed, every switch called out. Put the phone down and follow along.

Before you begin

  • Athletic clothing; supportive shoes if they suit your surface
  • Flat, dry, non-slip surface
  • Furniture, toys, pets and obstacles cleared away
  • Water bottle nearby

The one rule

Pain, dizziness, or feeling unwell — stop immediately.

Feeling warm or slightly out of breath is normal and expected. Sharp pain is not, and it's never something to push through.

Why it's all movement, no long holds

Everything here keeps moving, on purpose.

Long, held stretches belong at the end of a session — holding a stretch before explosive work can temporarily reduce your power. So the warm-up wakes the body with movement; the deep stretching waits for the cool-down.
